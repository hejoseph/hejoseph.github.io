Make your goal as a game and achieve them mindlessly.

Set the goals and tasks that you need to focus and achieve, and price tag them.

Then in the market, add tasks that you love doing, but that you should not be doing.

Price tag them as well. As you achieve your goals, you will earn coins, that you can use to buy task on the market.

Tasks bought are in inventory, that you can re-sell, or do the task. Enjoy !

Your data are saved in your browser locally, not online. Only you can see it !

Features v2.1:

- Important task can be dragndroped and re-oreder

- Steps can be minimized

- Header navigation

- Actions are logged

- A wallet graph and task done graph logs

- Tasks can be time-bound

- Tasks time-bound are displayed in a time table

- When doing Tasks time-bound, they will disappear from timetable

Features v2.0:

- Export/Import Data from a file : Objectives minimized or steps marked as done will be saved.

- Left click and maintain on objective to drag and drop. Top part of objective can be dragged

- Right click on objective to minimize or maximize it.

- Objective, step, and sub-step can be duplicated

- Objective can have different color

- Right click on objective to minimize or maximize it.

- Flag a task as important

- Important task list can be dragged and dropped

- Left click on the text of important task will scroll into objective frame

- Left click on top part of objective, will toggle mini-maximize

- Shortcut Ctrl+X will minimize all objective, and scroll to the top
